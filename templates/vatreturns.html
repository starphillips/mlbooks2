VAT Returns

VAT is an unavoidable part of doing business for most companies in the UK — but that doesn’t mean it has to be a hassle. MLBookkeepers provides a complete VAT service, from registration and record-keeping to return submissions and compliance reviews.

Whether you're on the Standard VAT Scheme, Flat Rate Scheme, or another tailored arrangement, we ensure your returns are filed accurately and on time, helping you avoid late submission penalties and interest charges. We also help you reclaim the VAT you're entitled to, so you’re not leaving money on the table.

Our VAT services include:

  • Registering your business for VAT
  • Advising on the right VAT scheme for you
  • Calculating and submitting quarterly VAT returns
  • Maintaining digital VAT records (Making Tax Digital compliant)
  • Liaising with HMRC on your behalf

Our support goes beyond form-filling. We help you understand what VAT applies to in your business, which rates to charge, and how to plan for VAT liabilities as part of your regular cash flow. We also track important VAT deadlines and keep your records clean and audit-ready.

If you’re newly VAT registered or just want to make sure everything is being handled correctly, MLBookkeepers can bring clarity and confidence to your VAT obligations.
