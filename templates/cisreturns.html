CIS Returns

If your business operates within the construction sector, you may already be aware of the Construction Industry Scheme (CIS). Under this HMRC scheme, contractors are required to deduct money from payments to subcontractors and pass it to HMRC. These deductions count as advance payments towards the subcontractor’s tax and National Insurance.

MLBookkeepers is here to take the complexity out of CIS. We’ll handle the registration process with HMRC if you haven’t done so already, and once registered, we manage your monthly CIS submissions — ensuring that all payments and deductions are reported accurately and on time.

Whether you’re a contractor, a subcontractor, or both (as many businesses are), our service ensures you stay compliant and avoid penalties. We’ll also keep you informed about any changes in CIS legislation that could affect your reporting obligations. If you’re unsure whether CIS applies to you, we’re happy to advise.

Staying on top of CIS rules can be a full-time job — let us do that part, so you can concentrate on your contracts and clients.
