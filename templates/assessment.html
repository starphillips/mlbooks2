Tax & Self-Assessment

For sole traders, freelancers, and company directors, Self-Assessment Tax Returns are a yearly responsibility that can feel confusing or stressful — especially when deadlines loom. At MLBookkeepers, we take the pressure off.

We’ll help you gather the necessary records, calculate your taxable income, apply any allowable expenses or reliefs, and submit your return to HMRC accurately and on time. We also check for any overpaid tax, and we make sure you’re only paying what you truly owe — no more, no less.

If you’re newly self-employed, we can assist with the registration process, ensuring that you’re compliant from the beginning. And if your tax situation is more complex (for example, multiple income streams or overseas income), we’ll help you make sense of what HMRC expects.

With over a decade of experience, MLBookkeepers is here to make your Self-Assessment process simple, smooth, and stress-free.
