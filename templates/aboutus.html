About Us

Let us give you some background you can trust

MLBookkeepers is a trusted, independently run bookkeeping business with over a decade of experience supporting small businesses, sole traders, and freelancers.

Founded by Michael, a qualified bookkeeper with over 10 years of experience in the industry before launching his own practice, the business has always been built on strong relationships, reliability, and personal attention. Many of Michael’s original clients chose to follow him after he went independent—and remain loyal to this day.

Offering a full range of services—including bookkeeping, VAT and tax returns, payroll, and credit control—MLBookkeepers tailors its support to each client's needs. We pride ourselves on delivering clear, accurate, and affordable financial management, so you can stay focused on running your business with confidence.

ML Bookkeepers is registered through the IAB (International Association of Bookkeepers) so you are assured that we are qualified and up to date with the ever changing practices at HMRC. We pride ourselves on a professional friendly approach in dealing with those matters important to you and your business.

Meet Michael

After nearly a decade working in bookkeeping for a local firm, I decided to take a leap and start my own business. I’d built strong relationships with my clients, and many of them trusted me enough to come with me on that journey. That trust meant everything—and still does.

Since then, I've spent over 10 years running MLBookkeepers independently, supporting clients with everything from day-to-day accounts to tax returns and payroll. I work closely with each client to understand their business and take the stress out of staying compliant.

Whether you're a freelancer, sole trader, or small company, I’d be happy to help you feel more in control of your finances.

Let’s talk—because good bookkeeping is about more than numbers. It’s about trust, clarity, and helping your business grow.
