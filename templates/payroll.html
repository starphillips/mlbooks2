Payroll

Running payroll can be time-consuming and complex, especially with constantly changing regulations and deadlines. At MLBookkeepers, we offer a comprehensive payroll service that takes the stress out of paying your staff. With extensive experience working with leading payroll software, including Sage Payroll, Xero Payroll, and Priority ERP payroll modules, we can ensure seamless integration with your existing systems and accurate, compliant payroll processing every time.

Whether you employ a single person or a large team, we handle every aspect of payroll processing with accuracy, efficiency, and compliance in mind. Our services are tailored to meet the needs of small and medium-sized businesses across the UK.

What We Offer:
  1. Complete Payroll Management
    2.

    We manage all aspects of your payroll cycle, from setting up new employees to processing leavers. Each pay period, we calculate gross pay, deductions (such as tax, National Insurance, pensions), and produce detailed payslips for your employees.

  2. RTI & HMRC Submissions
    3.

    We ensure full compliance with Real Time Information (RTI) requirements and submit payroll reports directly to HMRC on your behalf — helping you avoid penalties and keeping your records up to date.

  3. Pension Auto-Enrolment
    4.

    Navigating pension auto-enrolment duties can be tricky. We take care of pension assessments, contributions, and communication with both employees and your chosen pension provider, so you meet all legal obligations.

  4. Payslips & Year-End Reports
    5.

    Employees receive clear, accurate payslips (digital or printed), and we provide P60s and P45s as required. We also handle year-end processing and reporting, giving you a complete payroll summary for your records.

  5. Expert Support & Advice
    6.

    We stay on top of the latest payroll legislation, so you don’t have to. You’ll benefit from expert advice on topics such as holiday pay, statutory sick pay (SSP), maternity and paternity pay (SMP / SPP), and redundancy.

Why Outsource Your Payroll to MLBookeepers?

  • Save time — Focus on running your business while we handle the admin.
  • Ensure accuracy — Reduce the risk of costly errors and penalties.
  • Stay compliant — We ensure you meet HMRC and pension requirements.
  • Improve staff satisfaction — Pay your employees correctly and on time, every time.
  • Flexible service — Scalable payroll solutions as your team grows.
