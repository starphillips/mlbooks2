Debt Collection

Unfortunately, not every customer pays on time — and unpaid invoices can quickly build up, disrupting your cash flow and placing unnecessary strain on your business. MLBookkeepers offers practical, respectful, and persistent debt collection services designed to reduce that burden..

We take care of your follow-ups so you don’t have to. That includes issuing reminders, generating statements, and contacting clients who haven’t paid within their agreed terms. We also provide guidance on credit control strategies and best practices to avoid overdue accounts in the first place.

If the situation escalates, we can issue final demand letters and, when necessary, support you through the process of pursuing a County Court Judgement (CCJ). We do this thoughtfully — always aiming to protect your business reputation and relationships while ensuring that you're not left unpaid for services or goods you’ve delivered.

How we help improve your debt collection process:
  • Send invoice reminders promptly
  • Issue professional statements monthly
  • Chase overdue payments courteously but firmly
  • Provide final demand templates
  • Advise on next steps like CCJs, if required

By letting MLBookkeepers handle your debt collection, you can focus on running your business, knowing your income is being followed up promptly and professionally.
