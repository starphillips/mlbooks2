Credit Checks

At MLBookkeepers, we believe that credit checks can be a valuable safeguard when offering credit accounts to new or existing customers. It’s an important step that’s often overlooked in the excitement of onboarding a new client — but failing to assess financial stability can put your cash flow at risk.

There are several simple and cost-effective methods available that allow you to perform basic credit checks yourself, giving you greater peace of mind before entering into agreements. We’re happy to guide you through these steps if needed.

For larger or more critical accounts, we offer a comprehensive credit check service. This is ideal when you need to place significant trust in a customer and want assurance that their business is stable and unlikely to liquidate or close suddenly. As part of this service, we can also monitor changes in their credit status and alert you if there are any red flags — so you stay informed and protected at all times.
