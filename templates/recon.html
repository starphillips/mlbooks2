Bank Reconciliation

Bank reconciliation is one of the most important — and often overlooked — aspects of accurate bookkeeping. It involves comparing your business’s financial records with actual bank statements to ensure every transaction is accounted for. Mistakes, missing payments, duplicate charges, or even fraud can all go unnoticed if your accounts aren't reconciled regularly.

At MLBookkeepers, we handle your bank reconciliations thoroughly and consistently. Each month, we match your internal transaction records with your bank statement, identifying any discrepancies and resolving them quickly. This helps ensure that your accounts remain accurate, and it also gives you peace of mind knowing nothing is slipping through the cracks.

What we check during reconciliation:

  • Missed or duplicate payments
  • Bank charges or interest not recorded
  • Unmatched customer/supplier transactions
  • Manual entry errors
  • Unexplained cash withdrawals or deposits

We also help you put processes in place to prevent common reconciliation issues, like misfiled receipts or unrecorded transactions. With years of hands-on experience, we’ve developed a streamlined system that fits comfortably into your business’s routine, making your financial records easier to manage, easier to explain to HMRC if needed, and more reliable in general.
